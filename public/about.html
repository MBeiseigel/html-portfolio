About Me

Hi, my name is Melissa! I am a small animal veterinarian that recently decided to pursue web development. I have been a veterinarian since 2017, and have loved helping families keep their fur babies healthy. While there are many things about being a veterinarian that brought me joy, I have always felt that my natural skill set would be more effective in another line of work. My organizational skills, attention to detail, love for designing things, and a desire to maintain efficiency all led me to dip my feet into coding (that, and some encouragement from my amazing husband).

I am married to my husband, Cameron, who is the real brains in our family. We have two small boys that are going to be just as smart as him one day (someone help me).
